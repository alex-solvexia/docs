## How to change your SolveXia account details


1\. Log in to SolveXia.

2\. Click on your **username** and select **SolveXia Account**.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://help.solvexia.com/hc/article_attachments/11029849068943"/>
</Frame>
3\. Update the details.

4\. Click **Save**.
