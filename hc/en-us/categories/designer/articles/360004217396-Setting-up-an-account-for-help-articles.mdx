1.  Go to [**help.solvexia.com**](https://h8.t.hubspotemail.net/e2t/tc/VVzmNL9ljV4kW6dQdtX2Wc_t5W7zr5cm4q-0gQN880g3m3lGmwV1-WJV7CgMwZW3bxwm07h2S_hW1Ns2J041cbmyW3kMRtL24clsCW3T_X2G459Xj0W7xB65p8LWVQPW1rbWqS4d77n7W89Gfq01g4tL2W7GzFk58lv1gyW2X086J7vqCdfN8n5WDcysV5NW50mcZT9k4YjlVLtn5B6djcm7W9cVMSG965Hj7W6dMmPf6-kDDCW5TjbXw8w3Qp4W4Pwn--2VWC9GW9l472S10ZX4hW3Y5NnQ52LN9qVHh_1h6RPxK7W6_SjJ-7VX1_V39h41)
2.  Click on “**Sign in**”.
3.  On the sign-in page, select either “**Sign-up**” or if you have submitted a support ticket in the past, simply click on“**Get a password**”. When signing up, please use your work email address.
4.  Follow the prompts to set up your account.

Please note, you can also submit support tickets by emailing our team at [support@solvexia.com](mailto:support@solvexia.com) or calling us anytime on +61 2 7208 8567.
